Sumanyu Muku

Hi ! I am Sumanyu. I am a first year Masters student at NYU Courant studying Computer Science. I am currently working with Aishwarya Kamath, Nicolas Carion and Prof. Yann LeCun on a Neuro-Symbolic Solver for Visual Question Answering. Previously, I was a Research Fellow at IIT Delhi where I had the pleasure of working with Dr. Chetan Arora, Dr. Saket Anand and Dr. Vinay Namboodiri. I graduated with a Bachelors in Computer Engineering from Delhi Technological University in 2020. My current research interests include multi-modal reasoning, Neuro-Symbolic AI e.t.c, however I also have prior background in algorithmic fairness, medical imaging, adversarial attacks and defenses.
